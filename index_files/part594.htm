CYBER LAWS, CYBER FORENSICS, AND CERT‌

Today, the World Wide Web (WWW) is extensively used as an efficient and inexpensive means of distributing information, products, and services. While the Web infrastructure (also known as cyber infrastructure) has become a valuable international infrastructure for such activities, it has also introduced new types of crimes in our society, known as cyber crimes. Cyber crimes deal with crimes associated with the use of cyber infrastructure and information in the cyber space. Some of the efforts to deal with cyber crimes are described here.
