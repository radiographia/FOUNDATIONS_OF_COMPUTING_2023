CERT‌‌

CERT stands for Computer Emergency Response Team. A country's government establishes it as the nation's most trusted referral agency for responding to computer security incidents whenever they occur. It also assists the country's citizens to implement proactive measures to reduce the risks of cyber crime incidents. Its goal is to enhance the security of the country's communication and information infrastructure through proactive action and effective collaboration. Government of India has established a CERT, called CERT-IN, for citizens of India.
