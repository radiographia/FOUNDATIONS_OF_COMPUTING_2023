CPU ARCHITECTURES‌

CPU is the brain of a computer system. It performs all major calculations and comparisons, and also activates and controls the operations of other units of the computer system. Hence, no other single component of a computer determines its overall performance as much as its CPU. To evaluate a computer's capabilities, it is important to know how CPUs are internally structured,

how different CPUs differ from each other, and how to evaluate CPU speed. These and other related concepts are described here.
