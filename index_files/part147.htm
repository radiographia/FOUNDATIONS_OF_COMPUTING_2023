MEMORY STORAGE DEVICES‌

With the advent of efficient manufacturing technologies, price of memory chips has fallen considerably. As a result, storage manufacturers have introduced a new breed of electronic secondary storage devices (which do not use any mechanical component for operation). They often provide applications (like music player, etc.) on top of these storage devices for better marketability. Some of the popular ones are described here.
