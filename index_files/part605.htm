E-governance Applications‌

Any computerized application that deals either with government office operations or with delivery of services of a government office is an e-governance application. Few examples of such applications are:

1. Birth and death registration system. It records birth and death of citizens while issuing birth and death certificates.

2. Land records management system. It records status of all pieces of land along with history of sale and purchase deeds.

3. Railway reservation system. It enables citizens to book journey tickets.

4. Utility resources (electricity, water, cooking gas, etc.) billing system. It records status of consumed resources and payments received.

5. Insurance management system. It records details of all individuals holding insurance policies along with premiums paid/due and claims made.

6. Bank accounts management system. It records details of all individuals holding accounts with a bank along with deposits/withdrawals made, interest earned, etc.

7. Passports management system. It records details of all individuals holding a passport and foreign travels made by the individual.
