What is E-governance?‌

Use of Information Technology (IT) in service sectors has resulted in faster and easier delivery of much better services than before. Governments around the world have realized that they can also make use of IT to provide better services to citizens and business. As a result, many organizations have developed IT applications for use in various government departments. Electronic Governance (commonly known as e-governance in short) has emerged as a keyword to refer to all such IT applications. Often e-governance means an IT driven system of governance that works better, costs less, and is capable of servicing the needs of citizens and businesses better than a conventional system of governance. Its goal is to create a more responsive, productive, and effective administration. We also refer to e-governance as SMART governance because it aims at using IT to the processes of government functioning to bring about Simple, Moral, A ccountable, Responsive, and T ransparent governance.
