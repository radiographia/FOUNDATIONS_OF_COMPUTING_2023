COMMUNICATION PROCESSORS‌

The goal of network designers is to design networks in such a manner that data moves to the right place, at the right time, with minimum errors, and at the lowest possible cost. Network designers often use different types of communication processors to achieve these goals. Functions of some commonly used communication processors are described here.
