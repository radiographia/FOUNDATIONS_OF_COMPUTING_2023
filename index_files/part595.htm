Cyber Laws‌

Cyber law is a branch of law that deals with legal issues related to the use of computer and information technology. For example, it deals with issues such as:

1. Intellectual property with respect to software, hardware, and information in various forms in cyber space

2. Privacy of information with respect to authorized access and use of information

3. Various types of security attacks on the users and resources in the cyber space

4. Illegal e-commerce transactions

5. Freedom of expression on the Web

6. Jurisdiction of a cyber law, defining the boundaries (countries or states) in which it is applicable

Government of India has defined Information Technology Act (IT Act), which was first introduced in the year 2000 and has gone through several upgrades since then. It serves as cyber laws for dealing with cyber crimes in India.
