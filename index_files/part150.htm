Memory Card (SD/MMC)‌

Similar to flash drive, flash memory based cards (see Figure 5.26) are available as removable storage device in different types of electronic equipment. Some of the most popular ones are Secure Digital (SD) and Multimedia Card (MMC). Some other manufacturers have their own proprietary cards based on flash memory. Storage capacity of these cards range from 8MB to 128GB.

In addition to computers, various types of digital devices (such as digital camera and cell phone) use these cards. This also facilitates easy transfer of data from these devices to a computer for storage in the computer's hard disk or for further processing by the computer. For example, a user can transfer photographs taken by a digital camera to a computer for storage or editing. Each of these cards has its own interface and specific design features for use with certain types of devices.
