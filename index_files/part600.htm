Application Domains



Today, computers are used in almost all application areas. Their dominant use in some areas has resulted in coining of new terminologies, which refer to the usage of computers in those areas. Some of these new terminologies include E-governance, Geo-informatics, Medical Informatics, and Bioinformatics. These application domains have emerged as new professional career domains for many. Several educational and training institutions have started formal and non- formal educational programs to prepare professionals for manpower needs in these areas. A few popular application domains are described in this chapter.
